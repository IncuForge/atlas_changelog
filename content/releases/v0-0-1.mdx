## A first release

Atlas is now public: a desktop terminal with Compass, its graphical workspace for the independently installed Pi coding agent. It is still alpha, but the direction is real.

## The boundary

Pi keeps ownership of its agent, sessions, extensions, skills, themes, and packages. Atlas is the local space around that work.

## With credit

Atlas is an independent IncuForge project informed by Warp, Zap, Pi, and the Pi package community. We are grateful for the work that made this experiment possible.
